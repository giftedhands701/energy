4 REASONS WHY SOLAR IS A MUST

OWNERSHIP FROM DAY 1

Ultra Energy offers one of the best solar ownership programs in the state. From the very beginning, you are the owner of your solar system. As of now, you are renting power from the utility company, with no return investment. With solar energy, every energy bill you pay goes directly to paying off your solar system. Once you have the solar system paid off, you will no longer have an energy bill from the finance company or your utility company. Our ownership program allows homeowners to add instant equity to their home, whereas a lease puts a lien on the home.

FEDERAL AND STATE TAX INCENTIVES

When you choose to convert to solar energy, the government offers a nonrefundable tax incentive. Until the end of 2019, the federal tax incentive is 30% of the total system cost, with a state tax incentive of $1,000. Beginning January 1, 2020, the government will offer a federal tax incentive of 26% of the total system cost and a $1,000 state tax incentive. This nonrefundable state tax incentive could dramatically decrease your tax liability, while saving you money on your monthly energy bill.

$0 DOWN WITH A FLAT RATE LOAN TERM

The rates of your utility provider increase an average of 3%-6% every year. This means that, although consuming the same amount of energy, you will pay increasingly more. With our program, there is no upfront cost. We simply set you on a new monthly payment plan that is an average of 30% cheaper than your current utility company. Any cost that you would incur for installation, material, and labor is already included in your monthly payment. This monthly payment is a flat rate that will never change over the course of the loan.

CUSTOM SOLAR DESIGN SPECIFIC TO YOUR ENERGY NEEDS

Each individual homeowner has different energy needs. We take pride in ensuring that your needs are met. Our design team builds a solar project that is custom to your energy needs, based on your current energy consumption. Have a life change coming up that will affect your energy consumption? Don't worry! We can customize your solar project based on whatever needs you have. This ensures that every customer has just enough energy to meet their needs (to avoid overpaying for energy you don't use), and not so little that you are paying the utility company and the financing company. Your custom solar system allows us to guarantee that you will never see an electric bill from your utility company again.
